Hi! I'm Chrysostomos

I am currently a PhD Candidate at the Department of Digital Systems, University of Piraeus, Greece.

My research interests include Causal Inference, Causal-based learning, Cloud Computing, Edge Computing, Machine Learning, Deep Learning. My current research focuses on resource management on the edge using causal-aware machine learning methods.
